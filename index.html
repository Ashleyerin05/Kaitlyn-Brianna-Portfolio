My Approach

How I Build
Relationships

Luxury service isn't a script — it's a sensibility. It's the ability to read a room, anticipate a need, and make someone feel like the most important person in it.

i.

Listen Before You Style

Every relationship begins with understanding. I ask questions, notice details, and pay attention to what clients express — and what they don't.

ii.

Remember What Matters

A client's daughter's graduation. Their preference for relaxed fits. These aren't small details — they're the foundation of trust.

iii.

Create Comfort, Not Pressure

The best sales never feel like sales. I create an environment where clients feel free to explore, ask questions, and take their time.

iv.

Elevate Every Touchpoint

From the first greeting to the follow-up text days later — the in-between moments matter as much as the sale itself.
